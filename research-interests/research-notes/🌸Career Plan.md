### 🌸Career Plan



#### ✅1. Career Goals

My ultimate goal is to become a Security Operations Architect. My immediate goal is to secure a security operations role at a cryptocurrency exchange.

**Short-term Goal (during GD studies)**

I am targeting Security Engineer, InfoSec Web3, Security Operations, and DevSecOps roles at cryptocurrency exchanges. These roles require three core competencies: operations fundamentals (Docker/Kubernetes, AWS, CI/CD, Ansible/Terraform), security specialisation (access control, data encryption, security auditing, Linux troubleshooting), and Web3 bonus skills (penetration testing, smart contract auditing, OSCP certification).

I have a background in cybersecurity within the futures trading industry, with direct experience in the security logic of financial trading systems — closely aligned with exchange security roles. During the GD, I will focus on strengthening cloud platform skills, containerisation, and Web3 security knowledge, while building industry connections through events such as OWASP Sydney and Blockchain Security Meetup.

**Long-term Goal (3–5 years)**

To become a Web3 Security Operations Architect, responsible for designing highly available and secure distributed infrastructure, leading CI/CD automation and DevOps culture, managing security architecture across multi-cloud environments, and addressing Web3-specific threats such as smart contract vulnerabilities and private key management risks. Built on my existing financial security background, this is a clearly achievable growth path through the GD phase and 2–3 years of engineering experience.

#### ✅2. Why Studying in Australia Supports This Goal

**Point 1: Access to International Market Perspective**

Cybersecurity education in China focuses heavily on compliance and traditional industries, with virtually no courses or faculty specialising in Web3 security or cloud-native security. The international landscape is far more advanced in these areas. I have seen classmates participate in AWS-hosted conferences and internships that are simply not available domestically.

During my time in Australia, I plan to attend:

- [OWASP Sydney](https://owasp.org/www-chapter-sydney/) (registration: [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/owasp-sydney-chapter/))

- [Blockchain Security Meetup Sydney](https://www.meetup.com/blockchain-sydney/) (website: [blockchainsydney.org](https://blockchainsydney.org))
